About the West Zone Library

Periodical Section

The Periodicals Section is the place where needed information could be found in journals, magazines, newspapers, and other serial literature. There is a growing demand in the areas of newspapers, journals and magazines research. We have News papers and Magazines in Periodical section.

Circulation Section

This section includes novels and general books. Readers can borrow books which are available in this section for 15 days of duration. Most of Kannada novels are borrowed regularly in this section; few people come to this section to refer general books as well. Library circulation or library lending comprises the activities around the lending of library books and other material to users of a lending library. A circulation or lending department is one of the key departments of a library. The main public service point is the circulation desk. Circulation Section provides lending services and facilities for return book. Renewal of materials and payment of fines are also handled at the circulation desk. Readers can ask for basic search and questions for availability of books in Circulation section for lending.

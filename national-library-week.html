 National Library Week

National Library Week was established by Indian Library Association (ILA) in 1968, the National Library Week is celebrated on 14-20 November all over India and various programmes are being organised to create awareness about the importance of libraries.

National Library Week is an annual celebration highlighting the valuable role of libraries, librarians play in transforming lives and strengthening our communities.

Public Library in Karnataka celebrates National Library Week for "encouraging people to read in their increasing leisure time" to "improving incomes and health" and "developing strong and happy family life and with the theme "Wake Up and Read!"

It is a time to celebrate the contributions of our Public libraries and librarians and to promote library use and support. Creating awareness about library among all the public readers, especially for children, hence organising different competitions and exhibitions for public readers and children.
